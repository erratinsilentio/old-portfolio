My name is Kacper, nice to meet you...
Currently, im working in a restaurant (in Poland) on different positions - as a waiter, delivery guy and pizzerman. I really appreciate my environment. Honestly - I learned many skills and saw many things, met wonderful people - including my girlfriend.
Physically and mentally - i definitely felt pushed to my boundaries many times. It can be VERY demanding job. It teached me patience, focus, ability to organize my job. Quick decision making, as well as cooperating with others - communicating.

To find order in pure chaos.

But it has it’s downsides. Long working hours - from 12 to 11pm - not much time to engage in private life if you want to have a decent salary! There comes a moment when you realize what makes you happy in life. And that you need to make a change!
Coding has been on the back of my mind for a loooong time. I scratched the surface of it years ago in the college (had to drop out after first year due to personal reasons). I forgot about it, told myself it’s not for me because this or that.

One night i could not fall asleep. I was thinking about my future and all the things i wanted to do in life - travel, have pets, make music, have a job i love. A job that can fit into all of that. And in that pure moment, the thought of being a programmer appeared in my mind so clearly, I took it as a form of sign.

Sometimes your fate is changed in a blink of an eye, and I knew it was that moment.

My journey began on 1st August 2022 with The Odin Project.
